Instructions for downloading ICADisplay.gdb.zip from the PG&E Integration Capacity Analysis (ICA) Map
1. Go to https://www.pge.com/b2b/distribution-resource-planning/integration-capacity-map.shtml
2. You will be prompted for your username and password for your Wholesale Electric Power Procurement account. If you do not have an account yet, click "Register" and create an account.
3. After you have logged in, click "Download Spatial Data" in the upper right-hand corner.
4. ICADisplay.gdb.zip will download to your computer. Save it in this folder, i.e. under ccai-dac-grid\data\raw_data\ica
